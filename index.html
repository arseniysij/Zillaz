How and which Zillaz do I get?

You can buy a Zillaz in any of the following NFT marketplaces:

Magic Eden: https://www.magiceden.io/marketplace/zillaz_nft

Solanart: https://solanart.io/collections/zillaz

Opensea: https://opensea.io/collection/zillaz

Regarding which one, we considere all zillaz were created to be equal (besides Legendary collection), so go and grab the one that most suits your personality!
